Highmark Contact Center Strategic Assessment

Competitive Strengths

  • Industry-leading AI investment ($100M+)
  • 14,000+ employees using GenAI tools
  • Strong regional satisfaction scores
  • Integrated payer-provider model
  • Google Cloud strategic partnership
  • Laguna conversational AI for care mgmt

Development Areas

  • No announced CCaaS platform partner
  • Traditional contact center infrastructure
  • Limited voice AI integration
  • Digital experience below industry avg
  • High volume of basic inquiries (73%)
  • Multi-call resolution issues (33%)

Strategic Opportunities

  • Implement modern CCaaS platform
  • Expand self-service automation
  • Deploy proactive member engagement
  • Integrate voice AI assistants
  • Leverage Google AI for contact center
  • Create omnichannel experience

Strategic Recommendation

Highmark is exceptionally well-positioned for contact center transformation. With strong AI foundations, significant investment commitment, and proven employee adoption, implementing a modern CCaaS platform could dramatically improve member experience while reducing operational costs. The combination of Google Cloud partnership and potential CCaaS integration presents a unique opportunity to leapfrog competitors in digital experience delivery.
