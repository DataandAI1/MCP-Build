Company Overview

Healthfirst is New York's largest not-for-profit health insurer, serving 1.8 million members across New York City and Long Island. Sponsored by 15 downstate New York health systems, Healthfirst has pioneered value-based care models since its founding in 1993.

Key Differentiators

  • Community Focus: Serving 1 in 5 NYC residents, with one-third of NYC's Medicaid population
  • Value-Based Pioneer: 80% of insurance value under value-based contracts
  • Quality Leader: Only 5-star rated Essential Plan in NY for two consecutive years
  • Multilingual Support: Services in English, Spanish, Chinese, and Russian
  • Digital Innovation: Virtual Community Office and mobile app engagement

Market Position

Healthfirst operates in six areas across the New York metropolitan region: New York City, Long Island, Westchester, Rockland, Sullivan, and Orange counties. The organization employs 5,400 people and has experienced remarkable growth, with revenue increasing 45% from 2018 to 2021.

Technology Solutions & Digital Platforms

Virtual Community Office
Virtual platform that connects members with local Healthfirst representatives remotely, replacing physical walk-in community sites with digital engagement capabilities. Implemented in March 2020.
Workday Enterprise Management Cloud
Unified platform for finance, HR, payroll, and enterprise planning. Enabled 45% revenue growth from 2018-2021 with $2.5M annual financial benefit. Implemented in 2018.
My Healthfirst App
Mobile platform for member engagement, providing access to benefits, doctor search, and digital ID cards. Part of Healthfirst's digital-first strategy.
Schmitt-Thompson Triage Protocols
Evidence-based triage protocols for 24/7 nurse advice line, ensuring appropriate care recommendations and liability protection.
FHIR R4 Standards
Enables real-time API connections with hospital EHR systems for seamless data exchange and care coordination.
TeamSnap Partnership
Youth sports sponsorships across New York communities for brand awareness and community engagement.
